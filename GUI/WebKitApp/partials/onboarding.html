A quickstart guide

1 On the next screen, you'll see the boards ( ) available after initial sync. Press on the ones you want to subscribe.

3 When you click on a thread, you'll see posts you can upvote, downvote, reply and save. These actions are at top right of each post. You can (and should) also vote on the thread itself.

2 After you select your boards, go to threads view ( ). This view shows what's inside the boards you just selected.

If you ever get stuck, you can hover over buttons to get an explanation. Aether has quite a few more tricks up in its sleeve for your to discover, and more are added day by day.

You can find a more in-depth tutorial at the 'Tutorial' menu of Settings ( ). Enjoy!
